DESCRIPTION

i.ortho.photo is a menu to launch the different parts of the ortho rectification process of aerial imagery

i.ortho.photo -- Imagery Group
Initialization Options

1. Select/Modify imagery group (i.group)
2. Select/Modify imagery group target (i.ortho.target)
3. Select/Modify target elevation model (i.ortho.elev)
4. Select/Modify imagery group camera (i.ortho.camera)

Transformation Parameter Computations

5. Compute image-to-photo transformation (i.photo.2image)
6. Initialize exposure station parameters (i.ortho.init)
7. Compute ortho-rectification parameters (i.photo.2image)

Ortho-rectification Option

8. Ortho-rectify imagery files (i.ortho.rectify)
